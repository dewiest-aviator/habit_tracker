Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 1, 2025

Introduction

Habit Tracker (“the App”) is developed and published by Raijin Ryu (“we”, “us”, “our”). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

Information We Collect

  • Habit Data: All data you enter (habits, streaks, reminders) is stored locally on your device only.
  • Diagnostics: The App does not collect analytics, crash reports, or telemetry at this time.

How We Use Information

Your habit data is used solely to display progress, reminders, and statistics within the App. We do not transmit your data to external servers or third parties.

Data Storage & Security

All information remains stored on your device. If you uninstall the App, your data may be deleted. If you enable OS-level backups (such as Google Drive backup or iCloud), your data may be stored according to the platform provider’s terms.

Third-Party Services

No third-party services are used at this time. Should we add integrations in the future (e.g., analytics, push notifications, cloud sync), we will update this policy accordingly before changes take effect.

Permissions

The App may request OS permissions (such as notifications) strictly to provide core functionality. The App remains usable even if you deny non-essential permissions.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be published on this page, with the “Last updated” date changed accordingly.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 4, 2025

Introduction

Habit Tracker (the “App”) is developed and published by Raijin Ryu (“we”, “us”, “our”). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy describes what data the App handles and how it is used.

Information We Collect

  • Habit data (on‑device): Entries you create in the App (e.g., habits, streaks, reminders) are stored locally on your device. We do not receive this content on our servers.
  • Diagnostics & analytics (app telemetry): In certain builds we may collect limited usage and crash information to help us improve the App. This telemetry may include device model, OS version, app version, anonymized identifiers, event timestamps, and crash stack traces. We use Google Firebase services (e.g., Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics) to process this data on our behalf.

Environment note: Diagnostic/analytics collection may be enabled in testing builds (e.g., dev or staging) and in production releases as needed to ensure quality and stability.

How We Use Information

  • Provide core features such as tracking habits, reminders, and progress.
  • Monitor app performance and reliability, investigate crashes, and plan improvements.
  • Detect, prevent, or address technical issues and abuse.

We do not sell your information.

Data Storage & Security

Habit content remains on your device. If you uninstall the App, local data may be removed by the OS. If you enable OS‑level backups (e.g., iCloud or Google Drive backups), copies of your local data may be stored under the platform provider’s terms.

Telemetry handled by Firebase is stored by Google on our behalf and protected per Google’s security measures. We apply principle of least privilege and restrict access to diagnostic data.

Third‑Party Services

We use the following service providers to support the App:

  • Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics): app diagnostics, event metrics, and crash reports. Data is processed by Google on our behalf and may be stored on servers located outside your country.

If we add new integrations (e.g., cloud sync or push notifications), we will update this policy before changes take effect.

Permissions

The App may request OS permissions (e.g., notifications) strictly to provide core functionality. You can manage permissions in your device settings; some features may be limited if permissions are denied.

Your Choices

  • Analytics controls: Where supported, you can limit analytics collection from system privacy settings (e.g., advertising/analytics preferences). If we add an in‑app toggle, it will take precedence.
  • Access & deletion: Your habit data is stored locally; you can delete it from within the App or by uninstalling the App. For telemetry processed by Firebase, contact us to request access or deletion where applicable.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International Users & Legal Bases

Information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required by law (e.g., GDPR), we rely on our legitimate interests in operating and improving the App, and we will honor applicable rights to access, correction, objection, and deletion. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
